About Me

"Hey there, I'm Kartik, a robotics engineer with a knack for building robots that may or may not destroy humanity someday (just kidding!). My journey into the fascinating world of robotics began as a hobby during my engineering days at MIT School of Engineering. I started tinkering with ROS (Robot Operating System) for fun, and little did I know that it would lead me to craft a wide range of autonomous robots, from ground-based AMRs to drones, hexapods, and even autonomous buggies. So yes, while I may joke about world domination, my true mission is to bring robots to life and make the world a more innovative and entertaining place!"
